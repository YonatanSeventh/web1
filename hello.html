What is UNAI?

Universitas Advent Indonesia (UNAI) is a Higher Education Institution managed by the Seventh Day Adventist Church of the Western Union of Indonesia. The history of UNAI is that it was founded in Cimindi in 1929 which was originally called Opleiding School der Advent Zeading in the past, this school was founded for education of the evangelist of the Seventh-day Adventist Church.


Universitas Advent Indonesia has 6 Faculties :

  • 1) Faculty of Philosophy
  • 2) Faculty of Teacher Training & Education
  • 3) Faculty of Economics
  • 4) Faculty of Nursing
  • 5) Faculty of Mathematics and Natural Sciences
  • 6) Faculty of information Technology
